About us

Who we are

Our story

Packaging made in Liberia, for Liberia and beyond

J.S. PrintPack Ltd was founded to solve a problem every Liberian producer knows: quality packaging usually has to be imported — slowly and expensively. We manufacture flexible packaging and print custom branding locally, using modern flexographic technology, so businesses across Liberia and West Africa can get professional packaging without the wait or the import cost.
